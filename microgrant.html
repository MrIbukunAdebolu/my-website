Nexus Microgrant Programme

Empowering early‑career space and geospatial talent across Africa

Apply Now

About the Programme

The Nexus Microgrant Programme provides small, targeted financial support to early‑career individuals working in Earth observation, climate resilience, geospatial analysis, and space‑related fields. The goal is to remove modest financial barriers that prevent promising Africans from accessing opportunities, completing projects, or advancing their skills.

Why This Programme Exists

Across Africa, talented individuals frequently encounter financial constraints that limit their ability to participate in research, training, or professional development. These barriers are often small, but their impact is significant. This programme was created to address that gap through timely, mission‑aligned support.

Who Can Apply

  • Residents of Africa at the time of application
  • Students, early‑career professionals, or emerging researchers
  • Individuals working in Earth observation, climate resilience, geospatial analysis, environmental science, or space technology
  • Applicants with a clear, well‑defined activity requiring small financial support

What We Fund

  • Fieldwork and data collection
  • Conference attendance or presentation
  • Short courses, workshops, or training programmes
  • Prototype development or small technical projects
  • Community mapping or environmental monitoring
  • Research‑related travel or materials

Programme Cycles

  • Cohort A: January – June
  • Cohort B: July – December

How to Apply

Applications are submitted by email.

  • Email: info@nxspaceng.com
  • Subject line format:
    • Cohort A – Application for Nexus Microgrant – YYYY-MM-DD
    • Cohort B – Application for Nexus Microgrant – YYYY-MM-DD
  • Attach: Proposal, Budget & Financial Need Statement, Endorsement Letter

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply?
Applicants must reside in Africa and work in fields aligned with the programme’s focus.
Can I apply if I live outside Africa?
No, applicants must reside in Africa at the time of application.
Is an endorsement letter required?
Yes, an endorsement letter is required for all applications.
Can previous winners apply again?
No, previous grantees and members of their project teams are not eligible to reapply.
Can the funds be used for unrelated expenses?
No, funds must be used for the activity described in the proposal.
