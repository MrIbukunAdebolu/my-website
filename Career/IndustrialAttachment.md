---

**Location:** Hybrid (Primary base: Nigeria)

### About the Internship

Nexus Space Ltd is seeking motivated interns to support ongoing projects in **remote sensing**, **computer programming**, **Earth observation dashboard development**, **AI‑driven geospatial analytics**, and **cross‑domain collaboration**. Interns will contribute directly to active projects for Nexus Space Ltd and its partners, gaining hands‑on experience in applied Earth observation and data‑driven environmental intelligence.

This internship is ideal for students who want to deepen their technical skills, apply AI to geospatial problems, and gain structured mentorship in a professional, impact‑focused environment.

---

### Key Focus Areas

- **Remote Sensing:** Satellite image processing, geospatial workflows, environmental monitoring.  
- **Computer Programming:** Python, JavaScript, or other relevant languages for EO applications.  
- **AI for Earth Observation:** Applying machine learning and AI models to satellite data, classification, detection, and predictive analytics.  
- **Dashboard Development:** Building interactive Earth observation dashboards and analytical tools.  
- **Cross‑Domain Collaboration:** Working with multidisciplinary teams across engineering, data science, and environmental fields.
