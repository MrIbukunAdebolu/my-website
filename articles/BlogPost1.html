The African Earth Observation (EO) and space industry is on a steep growth trajectory, driven by increasing demand for data-driven solutions in key economic sectors. This growth signals a major shift towards leveraging advanced technology for sustainable development across the continent.

Key Application Sectors

EO data is transforming Africa's most critical sectors. Agriculture leads the way, using satellite imagery for crop monitoring and food security, followed by applications in climate resilience, resource management, and urban planning.

Regional Hubs of Innovation

Several African nations are emerging as leaders in the adoption and development of EO technologies, fostering ecosystems of startups, research institutions, and governmental space agencies to drive innovation.
