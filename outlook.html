2026 Strategic Priorities

Charting Africa’s Path to Space Leadership

As we step into a defining chapter for Africa’s space ecosystem, Nexus Space Ltd. is proud to present our 2026 Strategic Priorities—an ambitious roadmap that positions us at the forefront of space-led climate action and technical development.

1: High-Impact Thematic Areas

  • Climate Resilience: Advanced flood risk mapping and weather tracking.
  • Satellite Capability: Localized training in CubeSat mission design.

2: Strategic Programs for Growth

  • CubeSat Workshops: Strengthening local engineering expertise.
  • STEM Summer Camps: Inspiring the next generation.
  • Innovation Grants: Funding for EO research projects.

3: Strategic Partnership Focus

  • Academic Institutions: Standardizing satellite curricula.
  • Government Agencies: Data-sharing for policy support.
  • Global Industry: Direct technical mentorship.

Together, we are not just observing the Earth—we are shaping the future.
