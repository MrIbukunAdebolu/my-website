About Us

Nexus Space Ltd is a global consultancy at the frontier of space systems engineering, climate resilience, and planetary health. We specialize in designing and deploying small satellite platforms, integrating Earth observation data, and applying AI-driven analytics to solve real-world challenges across Africa and beyond.

As governments, space agencies, and research institutions increasingly invest in satellite development, Earth observation, and climate-smart innovation, Nexus Space Ltd stands ready as a trusted collaborator. Our team brings decades of experience in CubeSat design, geospatial intelligence, and environmental health modeling—making us uniquely positioned to support capacity building, technology transfer, and institutional strengthening across diverse contexts.

We work at the intersection of aerospace engineering, data science, and public health, delivering solutions that are scientifically rigorous and socially impactful. Our mission is to empower stakeholders with innovative, data-driven tools that leverage satellite systems and space-based analytics for sustainable development, climate adaptation, and planetary health.

What We Do
Satellite Systems Engineering: We design, test, and deploy CubeSats and microsatellites for scientific, commercial, and educational missions. Our capabilities include structural integration, thermal diagnostics, and deorbiting mechanisms for low Earth orbit platforms.

Climate & Environmental Intelligence: We apply remote sensing and GIS to assess coastal vulnerability, urban heat islands, and flood risks. Our models have informed climate adaptation strategies across West Africa and beyond.

Planetary Health & Data Science: We integrate AI, geospatial analytics, and epidemiological modeling to forecast disease outbreaks, assess air quality impacts, and guide climate-smart public health systems.

Our Research Impact
Nexus Space Ltd contributes extensively to the global body of knowledge through peer-reviewed publications, technical reports, and collaborative research. Our work spans satellite mission design, climate risk modeling, environmental health analytics, and AI-driven decision support tools.

Global Reach
With experience spanning Africa, Asia, and North America, we collaborate with universities, space agencies, and public health institutions to deliver solutions that are locally relevant and globally scalable.

Why Nexus Space Ltd?
Because we don’t just build satellites—we build capacity, resilience, and partnerships.

Our Leadership

Dr. Samuel Olumide Akande

Senior Scientific Officer, Climate & Space Applications Expert

Dr. Akande is a climate scientist and space applications specialist whose work helps communities better understand and respond to environmental challenges. With over a decade of experience in meteorology, satellite remote sensing, and geospatial analytics, he’s passionate about using science to improve lives—especially across Africa’s most vulnerable regions.

His research focuses on climate change impacts, crop yield prediction, and early warning systems. He’s developed tools like the EARWAC Dashboard and contributed to global climate reports. Dr. Akande holds a PhD in Meteorology and Climate Science and has published over 25 peer-reviewed articles.

Dr. Ibukun O. Adebolu

Space Systems Engineer

Dr. Adebolu is a space engineer with a passion for building resilient satellite systems and expanding access to space technology. With a PhD in Space Engineering and hands-on experience across Canada and Japan, he has led mechanical and systems engineering for CubeSats and microsatellites ranging from 1 to 100 kg.

His career spans roles in R&D project management, spacecraft design, and mission integration. He’s contributed to global satellite initiatives like the BIRDS and SPATIUM projects, and holds certifications in Six Sigma and Google Project Management.
